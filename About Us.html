About Us

Our History, Our Story

Our History

Founded in 2009 in Durban,South Africa, Aurelius began as a small family-owned establishment with a passion for North American and Italian cuisines, but later on became a versatile cuisine. What started as a humble local restaurant has grown into a beloved culinary destination known for its golden dishes.

Our founder, Marc Ndlovu, envisioned a place where authentic Italian and North American recipes could be shared with the community. Over the years, we've stayed true to our roots while innovating to bring our guests the best dining experience possible.

In 2018, we opened our second location in Umhlanga, and since then we've continued to grow while maintaining the quality and service that made us successful from the beginning.

Mission statement

At Aurelius, we are committed to delivering a unique and unforgettable dining experience by combining the finest elements of North American and Italian cuisines with global influences.

From our humble beginnings as a family-owned restaurant to becoming a beloved culinary destination, our mission is to bring together diverse flavors, exceptional service, and a warm atmosphere that makes every guest feel like family.

Core Values

Excellence in Flavor: We believe in using only the freshest, high-quality ingredients to create dishes that delight the senses and celebrate diverse culinary traditions.

Innovation: We constantly evolve our menu, blending classic and contemporary cooking styles to surprise and satisfy our guests' evolving tastes.

Warmth & Hospitality: We treat every guest with respect and care, ensuring they feel welcomed, valued, and part of the Aurelius family.

Sustainability: We are committed to sourcing ingredients responsibly and minimizing our environmental impact, ensuring a better future for our community.

Community & Family: Rooted in a deep sense of family, we foster strong relationships with both our team and customers, creating a space where everyone feels like they belong.
